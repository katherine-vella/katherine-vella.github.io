Rules

The game of Paper, Rock, Scissors is based on trust, skill, and determination. Each player must:
  • Make a fist with one hand.
  • Chant "Paper, Rock, Scissors!"
  • And make a move in the form of...
    • rock (hand in a fist)
    • paper (hand flat, palm down)
    • scissors (pointer and middle fingers extended, like peace sign)
And the winners are...
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors cut Paper
If the same item is thrown, it's a tie! To learn more, go to the World RPS Society
